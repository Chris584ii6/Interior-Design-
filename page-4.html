Basics

How to plan

Step by stepe

01. Clear the Clutter

 

Clutter can block the flow of positive energy (Chi) in your home. Start by decluttering each room, getting rid of items you no longer need or use. This creates a clean, open space that allows energy to flow freely.

02. Define the Bagua Map

 

The Bagua map is a Feng Shui tool that divides your space into nine areas, each representing different aspects of life (e.g., wealth, health, relationships). Overlay the Bagua map on your floor plan to identify which areas of your home correspond to these life aspects

03. Balance the Five Elements

 

  • Wood: Plants, wooden furniture
  • Fire: Candles, red or orange colors
  • Earth: Crystals, pottery
  • Metal: Metal frames, white or gray colors
  • Water: Fountains, mirror

04. Optimize Furniture Placement

 

Furniture placement is crucial in Feng Shui. Ensure that furniture is arranged to promote a smooth flow of energy. For instance, position your bed and desk to have a clear view of the door, but avoid being directly in line with it. This placement enhances a sense of security and control.

05. Enhance with Feng Shui Cures

 

Feng Shui cures are adjustments made to improve the energy in your space. These can include:

  • Mirrors: To expand space and reflect positive energy
  • Crystals: To attract and amplify energy
  • Plants: To bring life and vitality
  • Water Features: To enhance wealth and prosperity

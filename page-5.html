How to find your style

Step 1: Reflect on Your Lifestyle

 

Consider your daily routines, hobbies, and preferences. Your personal style should complement your lifestyle. For example, if you love hosting parties, a more open and social layout might suit you best.

Step 2: Gather Inspiration

 

Browse through magazines, websites, and social media platforms like Pinterest and Instagram. Save images that resonate with you search for styles that resonate with you - don't think too hard about this. Your interior should reflect your authentic self.

Step 3: Identify Your Favorite Colours

 

Favorite Colours Colours play a significant role in defining your style. Determine which colors make you feel happy and comfortable Create a color palette that you can use as a foundation for your design choices.

Step 4: Assess Your Current Space

 

Take a look at your existing furniture and decor. Identify pieces that you love and those you can do without. This will help you understand what elements are essential to your style and what needs to be changed.

Step 5: Experiment with Different Styles

 

Don't be afraid to mix and match different styles to see what works best for you. Try incorporating elements from various styles, such as modern furniture with vintage accessories, to create a unique look that reflects your personality.
